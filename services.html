Home Improvement Services

After years of use, it’s normal that you’ll find areas in your home that are damaged. Repairs should be done right away. Even if your house is still pretty new and you want to change some of the things there, we can remodel the area for you. Here in Joselito-Home Remodeling LLC we make sure you have lasting results. Our team of specialists in CT will provide you the improvement that you expect and more.

Interior Painting

Joselito-Home Remodeling specializes in professional interior painting, decorating and wallpaper services. Meticulous surface preparation ensures an optimal finish and exceptional durability. No job is too big or too small for our crew of painters. You can trust that the job's done right the first time, for the right price. Joselito-Home Remodeling offers a complete interior painting service.

Drywall

We specialize in Drywall installation as well. Drywall repairs, texturing, drywall water damage repair, acoustic ceiling removal, partition walls, drywall painting and much more.​

Carpentry

From custom closets to entry coat and shoe areas to under stairs storage we can help design and build a custom space that has functionality and elegance. Or simply updating interior doors and trim with crown and wainscoting our finish carpenters are true craftsman​

Kitchen

From a simple face lift of painting the cabinets and installing new counter tops to gutting you old kitchen creating a whole new layout with state of the art lighting, cabinets and appliance we will sit down and help you make the difficult decisions on planning a kitchen making sure every last detail is taken care of.​

Bathrooms

Update and upgrade an existing bathroom or add one on. Other than the kitchen a bathroom has the most decisions to be made when it comes to remodeling. Most older bathrooms are too small for today’s living standards. We start with the design and try to maximum space and see if we can take space from other rooms to create a spa like feel.​

Floors

The right flooring if its hardwood, laminate, tile, vinyl can make the difference in a space. With our partners across the state we can send you to one other their showrooms that is close to you so so they can help you pick the flooring that will make the space. After that all the scheduling and logistics is handles by us.​

Decks

Outdoor spaces are the new craze and the new decking products are flooding the market. We will sit down and discuss the difference between pressure treated, composite, Pvc, and exotic wood. Talk about different railing systems, cable, glass, traditional. Then help you design for functionality, comfort and budget.​

Plastering

Taping and floating drywall or “mudding” drywall is one of the most crucial elements to a successful drywall installation. At Joselito-Home Remodeling we have perfected the tape and float process resulting in a precision drywall installation at every job.

Interior Gutting

If you have a plan and need to remove some walls or remove everything inside to start fresh. Joselito-Home Remodeling can help you with interior gutting to ensure your idea can go as planned. Interior gutting is just one of many services we offer.
